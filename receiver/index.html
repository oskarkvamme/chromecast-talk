So, what is Chromecast?

Chromecast is..

  • A streaming device/dongle
  • Connected to your TV/Display (HDMI)
  • Video
  • Audio
  • WIFI
  • Cheap: 299 NOK/ 310 SEK

How can I stream to Chromecast?

  • Chromcast enabled web apps (Chromecast extension to chrome must be installed)
  • Chromecast enabled IOS apps
  • Chromecast enabled Android apps


Examples

  • Netflix
  • Youtube
  • Google Music

Setup

  • Get a device!
  • Run normal setup. Easy peasy lemon squeezy :)
  • NB! Remember to enable "Sende this Chromecast's serial number to Google..."
  • Register your device on https://cast.google.com/publish/#/overview (5 EUR)
  • Install chromecast extension for Chrome.
